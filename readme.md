## canvas tetris

## how to play
1p:     
    up: w   
    right: d    
    down: s 
    left: a 
    right turn: e   
    left turn: q    

2p:    
    up: keypad 8
    right: keypad 6
    down: keypad 5
    left: keypad 4
    right turn: 9
    left turn: 7
